Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Introduction

Bhagavad Gita Path ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Reading Progress: We store your reading history, bookmarks, and progress locally on your device
  • Preferences: App language, content language, notification settings, and audio preferences
  • Saved Verses: Verses you bookmark or save for later reading

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for analytics and crash reporting
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app (which verses you read, time spent)
  • Advertising ID: For personalized advertising purposes (can be reset in device settings)

Advertising

Third-Party Advertising

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the app free for all users.

What AdMob Collects:

  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Advertising ID (resettable in device settings)
  • IP address (for general location)
  • App interaction data
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

How AdMob Uses Data:

  • To serve relevant advertisements
  • To measure ad performance
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To improve ad quality

Your Advertising Choices:

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings ‚Üí Google ‚Üí Ads ‚Üí Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Advertising ‚Üí Limit Ad Tracking
  • You can reset your Advertising ID at any time in device settings

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Remember your preferences and progress
  • Send daily reminders (if enabled by you)
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Storage

  • All your personal data (reading progress, bookmarks, preferences) is stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit your personal data to external servers
  • Your data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties (except as required for advertising)

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data Collected: Device info, Advertising ID, IP address, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-out: Device settings (see Advertising section above)

Google Play Services

  • Purpose: App distribution and updates
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However:

  • AdMob: Automatically collects data for advertising purposes (as described above)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (through the "Reset Progress" option in Settings)
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising (through device settings)
  • Opt-out of notifications (through Settings)
  • Reset your Advertising ID (through device settings)
  • Uninstall the app at any time

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is stored locally using encrypted storage (Hive database)
  • Minimal transmission of data over the internet (only for ads)
  • No user accounts or login required
  • No collection of sensitive personal information

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in spiritual content. We do not serve personalized ads to users under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

AdMob may transfer data internationally as part of its advertising services. By using this app, you consent to such transfers in accordance with AdMob's privacy policy.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Advertising Data: Retained by AdMob according to their privacy policy
  • No server-side data: We do not store any data on our servers
